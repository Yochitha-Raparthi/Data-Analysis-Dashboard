# Sales Analysis Dashboard
The Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed using Microsoft Excel to provide comprehensive insights into sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product revenue, and delivery efficiency. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics in real time and make data-driven decisions through dynamic visualizations and filters.

The project analyzes order, customer, product, and delivery data to identify sales trends, top-performing products, revenue-generating categories, and customer purchasing patterns. Interactive slicers allow users to filter data by order date, delivery date, and occasion, enabling detailed analysis across different business dimensions.

Key Features
1. KPI Monitoring

The dashboard provides a quick overview of critical business metrics:

Total Orders: 184
Total Revenue: ₹5,72,342
Average Customer Spending: ₹3,110.55
Average Order-to-Delivery Time: 5.46 days

These KPIs help management track overall business performance and operational efficiency.

2. Occasion-Based Revenue Analysis

Analyzes revenue generated during different occasions such as:

Anniversary
Birthday
Raksha Bandhan

This helps identify high-demand events and supports targeted marketing campaigns.

3. Category Performance Analysis

Evaluates revenue contributions from product categories including:

Colors
Soft Toys
Mugs
Cakes
Plants
Sweets
Raksha Bandhan Gifts

The analysis highlights the most profitable product segments and supports inventory planning.

4. Revenue by Order Time

Tracks sales distribution across different hours of the day to identify peak ordering periods and customer purchasing behavior.

5. Product Performance Tracking

Displays the Top 5 Revenue-Generating Products, helping management understand customer preferences and optimize product offerings.

6. Geographic Sales Analysis

Identifies the Top 10 Cities by Order Volume, enabling businesses to focus marketing and logistics efforts on high-performing regions.

7. Time-Based Trend Analysis

Provides monthly sales trends and revenue patterns, allowing stakeholders to monitor business growth and seasonal fluctuations.
